### üéØ How It Works

1. **Start Interview**: Generate questions based on your desired role, company, and experience level
2. **Answer Questions**: Use voice recording to answer each question
3. **Get Instant Feedback**: AI provides scores and suggestions immediately
4. **Complete Interview**: After the last question, you'll be redirected to the report
5. **Analyze Performance**: Review detailed analytics and visualizations
6. **Export Results**: Download your report in multiple formats
7. **Start New Session**: Begin another interview to track improvement

### üí° Tips for Best Results

- **Be Specific**: Provide concrete examples and metrics in your answers
- **Manage Time**: Aim for 45-60 seconds per question for medium difficulty
- **Practice Regularly**: Compare reports across sessions to track improvement
- **Focus on Weak Areas**: Use the report insights to guide your practice
- **Share with Mentors**: Export PDF reports to get feedback from experienced professionals

### üìà Performance Metrics Explained

**Score Calculation**:
- **Completeness** (30%): Did you fully answer the question?
- **Clarity** (20%): How clearly did you communicate?
- **Relevance** (20%): How relevant was your answer?
- **Examples** (15%): Did you include concrete examples?
- **Conciseness** (15%): Were you concise and to the point?
