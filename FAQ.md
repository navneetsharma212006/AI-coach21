# FAQ - Detailed Interview Reports

## General Questions

### Q: When do I see the report?
**A:** The report appears automatically after you complete your last question and click "Complete Interview". You'll be redirected to the detailed report page.

### Q: Can I go back to the interview after seeing the report?
**A:** No, but you can export your report and then start a new interview. Click "Start New Interview" button on the report to begin again.

### Q: Does my interview data get saved?
**A:** Interview data is stored in your browser's session. Exports (PDF, JSON, CSV) are saved locally to your computer. We recommend downloading your report for permanent storage.

### Q: How many questions should I practice with?
**A:** Start with 5 questions (default) and increase to 10-15 as you get comfortable. Maximum is 20 questions per session.

## Report Features

### Q: What do the different charts show?
**A:** 
- **Line Chart**: How your scores changed across questions (identifies progress)
- **Bar Chart**: Time spent vs score earned (helps optimize speed)
- **Pie Chart**: How many questions fell into each performance level
- **Radar Chart**: Overall performance across all questions at once

### Q: Why is my average different from what I expected?
**A:** The average is calculated as: Sum of all scores √∑ Total questions.
For example: (7+8+6+9+8) √∑ 5 = 7.6

### Q: What does the "Performance Badge" mean?
**A:**
- ‚≠ê **Excellent**: 8-10 points (Very strong answer)
- ‚úì **Good**: 6-8 points (Solid answer with good structure)
- ‚óê **Average**: 4-6 points (Adequate but needs improvement)
- ‚úó **Needs Work**: <4 points (Significant improvement needed)

### Q: How is the score calculated?
**A:** AI evaluates based on:
- **Completeness** (30%): Did you fully answer the question?
- **Clarity** (20%): How clearly did you communicate?
- **Relevance** (20%): How on-topic was your answer?
- **Examples** (15%): Did you include specific examples?
- **Conciseness** (15%): Were you brief yet complete?

## Export Features

### Q: Which export format should I use?
**A:**
- **PDF**: For sharing with mentors or printing
- **JSON**: For technical analysis or data processing
- **CSV**: For analysis in Excel or Google Sheets

### Q: Why is my PDF taking long to generate?
**A:** PDF generation happens on your computer. Larger reports (20 questions) take 3-5 seconds. This is normal.

### Q: Can I edit the PDF after downloading?
**A:** PDFs are generated as images, so they can't be directly edited. Use JSON/CSV exports if you need editable data.

### Q: Where are my downloaded files?
**A:** Files are saved to your browser's default download folder (usually Downloads).

### Q: Can I share my PDF report?
**A:** Yes! PDF reports are great to share with:
- Interview coaches
- Mentors
- Career advisors
- Study groups

### Q: Why is my CSV not opening correctly in Excel?
**A:** 
- Make sure to open CSV with UTF-8 encoding
- Some special characters may need adjustment
- Try importing with "Text to Columns" feature

## Performance & Metrics

### Q: Why did my score drop from one question to the next?
**A:** Different questions test different skills and might have different difficulty levels. This is normal and helpful for identifying weak areas.

### Q: What's a good average score?
**A:**
- **8-10**: Excellent preparation
- **7-8**: Good preparation, some areas to improve
- **6-7**: Average preparation, needs practice
- **4-6**: Below average, requires focused practice
- **<4**: Significant practice needed

### Q: Should I aim for the same time on all questions?
**A:** Not necessarily. Some questions naturally take longer. Ideal range is 45-60 seconds for most interview questions.

### Q: What if one question has a much lower score?
**A:** This question type might be your weak area. Suggestions will help you improve. Practice similar questions and review the feedback.

## Data & Privacy

### Q: Where is my data stored?
**A:** 
- **Session data**: Your browser (temporary)
- **Preferences**: LocalStorage (your computer)
- **Exports**: Your downloads folder
- **No cloud storage**: Everything stays with you

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes:
- No server-side storage
- No tracking or analytics
- API calls use HTTPS encryption
- You control all exports

### Q: Can I delete my data?
**A:** 
- Session data: Clears when you close the browser
- Exports: Delete from downloads folder
- Preferences: Browser ‚Üí Clear data

### Q: What data is sent to the AI API?
**A:** Only:
- Your interview question
- Your answer text
- Time taken
- Your job role (for context)

No personal identifying information is required.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Report is blank or won't load
**A:** 
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check browser console (F12) for errors
4. Try a different browser

### Q: Charts not showing
**A:**

2. Scroll down to see all charts
3. Refresh page if frozen
4. Check JavaScript is enabled

### Q: Export button doesn't work
**A:**
1. Check pop-up blocker settings
2. Ensure sufficient disk space
3. Try different export format
4. Try different browser

### Q: PDF is missing content
**A:**
1. Entire page should be captured
2. Scroll through all sections before exporting
3. Try increasing browser zoom slightly
4. Export as JSON if PDF doesn't work
